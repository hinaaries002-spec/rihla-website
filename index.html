About

A calm and nurturing space for mothers.

Minaaz is an Amani Birth Doula, Advanced Amani Teacher, and Certified Advanced Doula serving families across the UAE since 2016, including Sharjah, Ajman, Ras Al Khaimah, and Dubai.

By the grace of Allah, she has supported over 90 families and welcomed approximately 96 babies, including twin births. She has been teaching the AMANI Birth Course since 2017 and is also a Silver Category Peer Breastfeeding Counselor certified by the Supreme Council of Family Affairs, Sharjah.

Her experience includes supporting natural births, water births, cesarean births, VBAC and VBAC2 births, preterm and twin births, natural breech births, and pregnancies with preeclampsia and gestational diabetes.

Minaaz’s work is rooted in compassion, continuous learning, and trust in Allah’s decree.
